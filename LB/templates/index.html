Setup your WIFI internet access

1. From a Windows or Linux computer load the SD card and edit the WIFI.txt file on /boot with your credentials.

Authorize the Raspberry Pi to send messages with your Telegram

2. Enter your phone number and click ok.

3. In the next 30 seconds you should receive a 5 digits key on your phone sent by Telegram, then click ok.

Set the username of your correspondent

4. Enter the username of your peer (person you want to talk to) (@username), then click ok.

Pi will reboot, wait two minutes. If everything worked well you should hear a Love bird singing. You can then hold the yellow button to send messages or short push to listen to messages.
